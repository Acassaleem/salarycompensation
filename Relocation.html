Financial Rationale

  • ● Depreciation: Adding ~23k miles annually significantly impacts the vehicle's resale value beyond standard expectations.
  • ● Maintenance: 4WD systems and SUV tyre specifications carry higher service premiums.
  • ● Tax Efficiency: Unlike business travel, commuting is a post-tax expense, meaning £1 of cost requires ~£1.39 of gross earnings.

HMRC Compliance Note

The Approved Mileage Allowance Payment (AMAP) rate of 45p/mile is the industry benchmark for vehicle use. Because this is a permanent site change, standard tax-free reimbursement is unavailable. The proposed "Gross Increase" ensures that the employee is not financially disadvantaged by the business's decision to relocate the workspace.
